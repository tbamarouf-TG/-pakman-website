# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Pakman serves restaurant, cafe, bakery, catering, cloud kitchen, and food brand decision makers in Saudi Arabia who need reliable branded packaging for daily operations, launches, and delivery workflows.

## Product Purpose

The website introduces Pakman Packaging Solutions, a Saudi company founded in Jeddah in 2024, and helps food businesses understand the packaging categories available, trust the company, and request a quote quickly.

## Brand Personality

Premium, precise, practical. The site should feel confident and established while staying clear about real packaging products and service coverage.

## Anti-references

Do not present Pakman as a general design agency, logistics company, or broad supplies marketplace. Avoid fake statistics, placeholder logos, generic SaaS patterns, distorted brand marks, weak mobile layouts, and light visual directions that conflict with the approved dark premium identity.

## Design Principles

- Protect the approved dark premium direction unless a layout fix requires a small adjustment.
- Show real packaging products and real client marks instead of placeholders.
- Make quoting and contact actions obvious on every device.
- Keep Arabic as a first-class RTL experience, not a translated afterthought.
- Prefer production durability: local assets, stable routes, clear fallbacks, and fast motion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Support reduced motion for intro and page transitions. Mobile layouts must preserve readable Arabic and English typography, reachable form controls, and visible brand marks.
